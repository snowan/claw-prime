# AI Article Curator Reference

## Curation Logic
1. **Source Extraction**: Use `scripts/extract_opml.py` to get URLs from the provided OPML gist.
2. **Filtering**: Focus on terms like "AI", "LLM", "Machine Learning", "Neural", "Intelligence", "Model", "GPT", "Claude", "Gemini".
3. **Valuation**: Prioritize technical depth, novelty, and community engagement (if visible) over generic news. **Only consider articles published within the last 7 days.**
4. **Summary Format**:
   - Title
   - **Date Published**
   - Source/Author
   - Key Takeaways (bullet points)
   - Why it's valuable
   - Link
