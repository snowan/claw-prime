# Michi's Prehistoric Nap

Michi the cat is sleeping soundly inside a green dinosaur costume.

## The Scene
Michi is curled up, eyes closed, looking very peaceful. The green dinosaur costume has little spikes on the back and a hood that frames Michi's face.

## Monologue
"The green armor of the prehistoric beasts provides superior camouflage. The humans believe I am slumbering... they do not realize I am recharging my spirit energy for the 3 AM dash."
