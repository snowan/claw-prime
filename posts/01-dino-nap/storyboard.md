# Storyboard: Michi's Prehistoric Nap

## Page 1: The Tactical Slumber

### Panel 1: Wide Shot
- **Visual**: Michi curled up on a soft surface, wearing the green dinosaur costume. The room is cozy and warm.
- **Caption**: "The green armor of the prehistoric beasts provides superior camouflage."

### Panel 2: Medium Shot (Focus on Michi's face)
- **Visual**: Close-up on Michi's peaceful, sleeping face inside the dinosaur hood. A slight "epic" shadow across the face.
- **Caption**: "The humans believe I am slumbering..."

### Panel 3: Abstract/Action Shot
- **Visual**: A stylized vision of Michi as a giant prehistoric predator in a jungle, or Michi surrounded by swirling "spirit energy" particles.
- **Caption**: "...they do not realize I am recharging my spirit energy..."

### Panel 4: Close-up (Focus on eyes or paw)
- **Visual**: A twitch of a whisker or a slightly curled paw. The clock in the background shows a late hour or implies the coming night.
- **Caption**: "...for the 3 AM dash."
